Public Relations Testimonies


Here we have two different members sharing their experiences in the Bloxco's PR Department. OfficialNav, the director of PR, and AGuyNamedKevin, the Marketing Lead.

"After taking a short break from the corporate team, I first joined PR in June of 2024. PR welcomed me with open arms, but unfortunately due to internal conflicts, I was forced to transfer out. I transferred to the HR department and stayed there until I was invited by Sapro, Chase, Owen, and Marc to transfer back into PR as the enagagement lead. I helped open the department and eventually became the PR director. PR has been a crucial part of my Bloxco experience, and I truly grateful to the people who allowed me to be in it."

OfficialNav
Director of PR

"Being in PR has taught me a lot. I started as a marketing intern not knowing much about videos, but with training and help from my team, I improved. Now my videos are featured on Bloxco’s TikTok and YouTube. After my internship, I was promoted to Junior Corporate, then later to Head Corporate – Marketing Lead, skipping Senior Corporate. I now train new interns, read applications, and help with other PR tasks. Bloxco has been super welcoming, and it’s been an awesome journey!"

AGuyNamedKevin
Marketing Lead
